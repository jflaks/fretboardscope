Instructions

This tool is designed to help learn relationships between patterns and scales on the fretboard. You begin by selecting a key or chord to constrain the selections of scales which you want to display, then choose a specific scale. By choosing a different scale on each selector, you can visualize how the patterns relate and how to move between them, for example playing close groupings of notes from each in different bars.
